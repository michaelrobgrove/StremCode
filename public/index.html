How to install
1
Click Install in Stremio — Stremio will open and ask to confirm the addon installation.
2
Or copy the Addon URL and paste it manually in Stremio → Add-ons → Community Add-ons → Install URL.
3
Your XC library will appear in Stremio under Discover → XC Movies and XC Series.
🔒 Security Note
Your credentials are AES-256-GCM encrypted inside the addon token. They are never stored on any server. Only your Stremio client decodes them on-demand.
